From this point on, the issue is not so much to find great works but to find great performances of such works. So some modern masterpieces are not listed here because there is no great recording to recommend. There are, however, great live performances around the world. Only a tiny minority gets recorded by the music labels because the market for modern music is obviously not as big as the market for famous symphonies.
See this page, this page and this page for a list of compositions.
